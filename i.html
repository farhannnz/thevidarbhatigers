Our Purpose

Our Mission & Vision.

Our Mission

Our mission is to unite motorcycle enthusiasts through safe, organized, and memorable riding experiences that foster friendship, learning, and personal growth. We are committed to promoting road safety, encouraging responsible riding practices, supporting community welfare initiatives, and preserving the spirit of adventure. Every ride reflects our values of brotherhood, discipline, and the true strong spirit.

Our Vision

To establish The Vidarbha Tigers Riding Community as a trusted and respected organization that promotes responsible motorcycling, adventure, and lifelong team spirit. We aspire to create a culture of discipline, safety, and mutual respect while inspiring riders to explore new horizons with purpose. Through leadership, community engagement, and ethical conduct, we aim to set a benchmark for riding communities across the region.
