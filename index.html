About The Community

More Than Riders.
We Are A Tribe.

The Vidarbha Tigers is more than a riding community, it's a tribe of passionate riders united by adventure, brotherhood and the love of the open road.

Born in Vidarbha, we bring riders together to explore new roads, experience unforgettable journeys and create connections that go beyond motorcycles.

Different bikes. Different stories. One roar.
